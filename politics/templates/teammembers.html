Alex Haynes

Founder, Chief Developer, Data Acquisition

As founder I am responsible for the overall vision and goals of the company. In addition to that I have been the chief developer since I started developing this website on my own in 2015. Travis Cook

Designer, Analyst, Manager

Ever since I could think for myself I’ve known that American Politics is overly complicated, too hard to understand, and far too time consuming for the average citizen to have their voice heard in our noisy political system. I truly believe The Glass Capitol will be the first great step towards realizing a less monotonous, more efficient political system where effective communication and action takes place between citizens and their representatives. I work directly with the president collaborating on product ideas and concepts, and serve as an information conduit to aid in guiding company activity on various time-scales. Additionally, I utilize Industrial Engineering techniques to optimize the site layout and navigation, making it possible for new and recurring users to intuitively get where they want as fast as possible.
